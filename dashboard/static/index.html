Weather Edge

Automated Weather Market Trading Terminal

How This Works

This bot scans Kalshi weather markets every 15 minutes, comparing market prices against a multi-model ensemble forecast (GFS, ECMWF, CFS, HRRR). When the model detects meaningful edge — the difference between our probability estimate and the market price — it sizes positions using a modified Kelly criterion and places limit orders.

The forecast pipeline fuses up to 4 weather models with confidence weighting and bias correction. Position sizing uses a sigmoid-dampened fractional Kelly formula that accounts for model confidence, fee drag, and bankroll constraints.
